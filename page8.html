Fun facts about this planet:

  • Earth's Twin: Venus is often called Earth's "sister planet" because they are similar in size, composition, and proximity to the Sun.
  • Hottest Planet: Venus has the hottest surface temperatures in the solar system, averaging around 900Â°F (475Â°C), due to a thick atmosphere that creates a runaway greenhouse effect.
  • No Moons: Unlike Earth, Venus has no natural satellites or moons, making it one of the two planets in the solar system without any.
  • Slow Day: A day on Venus (one complete rotation on its axis) takes about 243 Earth days, while a year (one complete orbit around the Sun) is only about 225 Earth days.
  • Volcanic Landscape: Venus has many volcanoes, some of which may still be active. Its surface is covered with plains, mountains, and large volcanic structures.
