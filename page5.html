Fun facts about this planet:

  • Oceans Galore: About 71% of Earth's surface is covered in water, with the Pacific Ocean being the largest body of water.
  • Spinning Speed: Earth spins at around 1,000 miles per hour (1,600 km/h) at the equator, causing day and night.
  • Changing Continents: Earth's continents are constantly shifting due to plate tectonics, a process that has created mountains, earthquakes, and volcanoes over millions of years.
  • Diverse Ecosystems: Earth is home to a vast range of ecosystems, from deserts to rainforests, polar ice caps to deep oceans, supporting millions of species.
  • Atmospheric Shield: Earth's atmosphere acts as a protective shield, blocking harmful solar radiation and burning up most meteoroids before they hit the surface.
